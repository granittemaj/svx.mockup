Company

Future-proof by design.

SVX is an advanced materials technology company on a mission to make the materials that power electrified transport, grid storage, and strategic systems cleaner, cheaper, and made closer to home.

Mission

Cleaner, more efficient pathways for critical materials.

We work with leading battery manufacturers and ecosystem partners to modernize how the next generation of plants operate, with a process that is precursor-free, uses domestic feedstocks, and supports regionalized supply chains. By removing process steps and relying on off-the-shelf equipment, we reduce capital needs, simplify scale-up, and cut environmental impact.

VALUE / 01

We build for the real world

We favour commercially proven equipment, domestic inputs, and fewer process steps, so what we develop can actually be built and scaled, not just demonstrated.

VALUE / 02

We future-proof

We design materials and manufacturing for the supply chains the West needs next: resilient, domestic, and independent of concentrated overseas production.

VALUE / 03

We own the outcome

From synthesis and validation to customer qualification, we hold ourselves to measurable progress and see the work through to commercial production.

VALUE / 04

We simplify

Real advantage comes from removing complexity, fewer steps, less capital, and lower impact, not adding it. We keep reducing until the process is genuinely better.

Leadership

Real-world execution, lab to commercial.

We pair deep technical capability with practical business leadership and guidance from a strong board and advisory network.
