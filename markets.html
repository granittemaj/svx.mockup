Markets

One platform. Three strategic markets.

SVX is building a more secure foundation for the battery economy. Our manufacturing platform supports the cathode-material needs of national security, energy storage, and electric mobility through efficient regional production.

01 / Defense

Defense & National Security

Critical defense systems require battery materials that are secure, traceable, and independent of adversarial supply chains. SVX enables domestic cathode production using locally available inputs and equipment designed for regional scale.

  • Domestic and allied feedstock pathways
  • Reduced reliance on foreign entities of concern
  • Resilient production for mission-critical applications
  • Compatible with defense and national-security sourcing priorities
DEFENSE · STRATEGIC
02 / Energy

Grid & Data Center Energy Storage

Rising electricity demand from AI, data centers, manufacturing, and grid electrification is accelerating the need for affordable, domestically produced energy storage. SVX offers a lower-capital pathway to scale the cathode materials required for reliable, secure storage deployment.

  • Starting with the first product: high-performance LFP cathode active material, the dominant chemistry for BESS (battery energy storage systems)
  • Lower-capital regional manufacturing
  • Supply security for rapidly growing electricity demand
  • Closed-loop and recycled-input pathways
GRID · STORAGE
03 / Mobility

Electric Mobility

Reaching cost parity with internal-combustion vehicles requires continued reductions in battery cost without compromising performance or supply security. SVX provides lower-cost, FEOC-compliant cathode active materials designed as a drop-in solution for existing battery manufacturing systems.

  • Lower cathode and cell cost
  • FEOC-compliant supply chains
  • Drop-in compatibility with existing electrode and cell manufacturing
  • Domestic production supporting automotive scale-up
EV · MOBILITY
