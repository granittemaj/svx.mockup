Our belief

Nature already solved energy. We translate it into the materials that power what comes next.

From renewable, domestic inputs to a waterless process that respects the planet, SVX is built on one conviction: the cleanest, most resilient path forward is designed with nature, not against it.

The stakes

The cathode is the bottleneck to scaling batteries in the West.

Today's critical materials are optimized for an overseas supply chain, costly, energy-intensive, and hard to scale domestically. The cathode is the single largest driver of both cost and carbon.

Demand is climbing toward thousands of GWh across mobility, grid storage, and defense. Meeting it requires a step change in how cathode material is made, not an incremental fix.

The technology

A drop-in material, engineered for resilient manufacturing.

Our proprietary waterless process produces cathode active material with fewer steps, smaller reactors, and off-the-shelf equipment, cutting the capital and carbon out of every plant.

01 / COST

Lower cost

Fewer process steps and a broader range of inputs, including recycled metal oxides, drive cathode cost down.

02 / CAPITAL

Lower capital

Smaller reactors and off-the-shelf equipment massively reduce the capital needed to build and scale a plant.

03 / CARBON

Lower carbon

A waterless route removes 20B+ gallons of wastewater annually and charts a path to net-zero EVs.
