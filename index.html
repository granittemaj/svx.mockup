Our belief

The energy transition needs a better manufacturing foundation.

Electrification cannot depend on costly, resource-intensive processes and concentrated overseas supply chains. SVX is building a more efficient pathway to cathode active materials using domestic inputs, fewer process steps, and a waterless manufacturing platform designed for regional scale.

The stakes

The cathode is the bottleneck to scaling batteries in the West.

Battery demand is scaling faster than resilient Western supply chains can be built. Cathode active materials remain one of the most concentrated, capital-intensive, and environmentally burdensome parts of battery production.

Conventional manufacturing was designed around large overseas industrial clusters, complex precursor supply chains, and significant water and infrastructure requirements. Replicating that model in the United States and allied markets is expensive, slow, and vulnerable to geopolitical disruption.

SVX is developing a more practical pathway: direct cathode manufacturing from domestic inputs using fewer process steps and commercially available equipment.

The technology

A drop-in material, engineered for resilient manufacturing.

Our proprietary process produces advanced cathode active materials without a conventional precursor stage or process water. Fewer steps, commercially proven equipment, and a smaller plant footprint reduce the cost, capital, and environmental burden of domestic production while enabling faster, more efficient scale-up.

01 / COST

Lower cost

Fewer process steps and lower-cost domestic inputs reduce conversion costs and simplify the supply chain.

02 / CAPITAL

Lower capital

Commercially available equipment, a simplified plant design, and a smaller footprint reduce the capital required to establish regional CAM production.

03 / IMPACT

Lower environmental impact

A waterless, closed-loop process reduces energy use, waste, and manufacturing emissions.

04 / SCALE

Built to scale

A simplified process based on commercially proven equipment reduces scale-up risk and enables modular regional production.

Markets

One platform. Three strategic markets.

SVX technology is chemistry-agnostic and industry-agnostic, deployable wherever lithium-ion batteries power the energy and strategic future.

01 / Defense

Defense & National Security

Critical defense systems require battery materials that are secure, traceable, and independent of adversarial supply chains. SVX enables domestic cathode production using locally available inputs and equipment designed for regional scale.

02 / Energy

Grid & Data Center Energy Storage

Rising electricity demand from AI, data centers, manufacturing, and grid electrification is accelerating the need for affordable, domestically produced energy storage. SVX offers a lower-capital pathway to scale the cathode materials required for reliable, secure storage deployment.

03 / Mobility

Electric Mobility

Reaching cost parity with internal-combustion vehicles requires continued reductions in battery cost without compromising performance or supply security. SVX provides lower-cost, FEOC-compliant cathode active materials designed as a drop-in solution for existing battery manufacturing systems.
