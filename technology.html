The technology

The cathode, re-engineered from the molecule up.

A precursor-free, waterless route to cathode active material, built on off-the-shelf equipment so it scales without the capital and carbon penalties of conventional production.

Why the cathode

The largest cost and carbon driver in every battery.

Solving the cathode is the highest-leverage move available to anyone trying to scale batteries affordably and cleanly in the West.

The process

Four steps. No precursor. No process water.

STEP 01

Domestic feedstock

Source from a broad range of inputs, including recycled metal oxides and locally available minerals.

STEP 02

Waterless synthesis

Form cathode active material directly, skipping the capital- and carbon-heavy precursor stage.

STEP 03

Off-the-shelf scale-up

Run in compact, proven reactors that de-risk and accelerate the path to volume.

STEP 04

Drop-in qualification

Deliver NMC, LFP or NCA that integrates straight into existing cell and pack lines.

Three outcomes

Lower cost. Lower capital. Lower carbon.

01 / COST

Lower cost

Fewer process steps and broader, cheaper inputs drive cathode cost down and unlock more affordable batteries across every market.

02 / CAPITAL

Lower capital

Smaller reactors and standard equipment cut the upfront capital needed to build a plant, making regional production viable.

03 / CARBON

Lower carbon

Eliminating process water removes 20B+ gallons of wastewater annually and opens a path to net-zero EVs with recycled inputs.
