ENGINEERING · FULL-TIME · SF BAY AREA

Engineering Technician

Apply for this role

Support synthesis, validation, and scale-up of our cathode active material process in the lab and on the pilot line. You will work hands-on with our process and engineering teams to run experiments, capture data, and help move our precursor-free, waterless route from bench toward commercial volumes.

What you'll do

  • Run synthesis and validation experiments and record clean, reliable data.
  • Operate and maintain lab and pilot-line equipment.
  • Support process engineers on scale-up trials and troubleshooting.
  • Help document procedures and improve lab safety and throughput.

What we're looking for

  • Hands-on lab or pilot-plant experience in chemistry, materials, or chemical engineering.
  • Care for accuracy, safety, and reproducible results.
  • A collaborative, roll-up-your-sleeves approach in a small team.
